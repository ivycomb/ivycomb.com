About

Hi, I'm ivycomb

Musician, YouTuber, Twitch Streamer

Born in Toronto, ON, and currently residing in Vancouver, BC, Ivy has dedicated their time to learning as much about all that you can create digitally in a wide variety of fields.

With a diverse skillset; music production, singing, voice acting, photography, video editing, motion graphics, animation, programming, and more. Using these skills, Ivy has created a variety of niche works over the years, culminating in a combined following of over 500,000 across their channels!

Ivy has produced music for Regretevator, is releasing their first studio album with Stephanafro, worked for Linus Tech Tips as a writer, worked with Monstercat artists on video projects, among many other small projects and achievements throughout the years.
